      • About The Club

      A college is not all by itself; it stands on three crucial pillars, namely ACADEMICS, SPORTS and Cultural activities. There is immeasurable hidden talent within the college, and what is talent that remains unacknowledged. It would be indignity to both, the talent and the talented. So, Vista Club aims at finding those hidden artists and giving them the requisite limelight. To provide a platform to the students to show their hidden talent. To promote UNITY IN DIVERSITY within college. For both college and students to gain recognition in various fields apart from academics. For participants to gain confidence for the long run, in short, for personality development. .
